About Us

Who we are

Golden Nest is a family-run farm raising free-range chickens to provide fresh eggs and quality poultry meat. We prioritize animal welfare, food safety, and community support.

What makes us different

Free-range practices, minimal chemical inputs, and local sourcing. Our products are traceable and handled with strict hygiene standards.

Mission & Vision

Mission

“Nourishing communities with quality poultry and eggs, sustainably and with care.”

Vision

“To be a leading, trusted brand in poultry and egg production, setting standards for excellence and sustainability.”
